# About
## Hi!

I'm Aryan. I graduated from IIT (BHU) Varanasi. I build retrieval, agentic, and inference systems for LLMs, work that usually starts as a hard constrain surface and ends as production infra.

Currently an AI Engineer at Sezzle, and co-author of DiscoveryBench (https://openreview.net/challenge?redirect=%2Fforum%3Fid%3DvyflgpwfJW) (ICLR 2025). Previously, Linux Foundation Mentee (https://mentorship.lfx.linuxfoundation.org/project/4550c85f-2451-47bd-9873-94b60f9cf701) 2026, where I built an AI assistant for the chaos engineering tool krknctl (https://github.com/krkn-chaos/krknctl-assist).

Blogs, thoughts, and things I'm working on."
